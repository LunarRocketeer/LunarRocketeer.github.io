Twitch Livestream Graphics

2021

Wanting to get into Twitch streaming, I designed a basic graphics package with a colorful sci-fi feel, with the background based on the bridge of the Enterprise D. I used Adobe Illustrator to do the images, Adobe After Effects to animate a custom popup, Streamlabs plugins to display chat and alerts, and OBS to put it all together with customizable pre-stream text, and transitions and animations between everything. The best way to see it is in motion, and since I rarely go live on Twitch, I think a sample YouTube video will be a little more conveinent for you. I've got more sketches and plenty of room in this package for additional graphics later, if I decide to further persue streaming and have need for more.

The starfield, notification sound, and the video game Minecraft were all made by other people.

City of Bellevue's Lake to Lake Trail

2019-2020

I did design work for the City's trail project. Most of this work was just done to present our other work (see Geography, Planning, Data page for details, and for full copies of the presentations), but a few designs were for elements of the trail itself. Roughly in order of apperance and design date, I created: diagrams relaying information about the Atlanta Beltline trail, a logo concept for the Lake to Lake trail, the overall powerpoint design for our Winter quarter presentation, icons used in said presentation (two sampled here), and illustrations - the first depicted here shows a concept for a "Meditative Gazebo", the second, a slightly more abstract imagining of the trail.

SARP Merchandise

2018

I made several designs for shirts, patches, buttons, and business cards as part of the business team for the Society of Advanced Rocket Propulsion. None of them went into production, but I remain proud of the work. Each constellation tangentially relates to its respective subteam, and all come together into one starfield for the team patch. Looking back, I might have chosen a few different constellations, but these still look pretty cool.

I also designed the look for our sponsorship packet, which goes out to companies interested in donating to the team. You can click the sample to the left to access the document. And go donate to the current SARP team, if you find my nice colors convincing.
